# Gulf Coast Beach Vibes — Content Plan
*Last updated: 2026-03-29*

## Geographic Scope
**Dauphin Island, AL → Panama City Beach, FL only.**
This site covers the Alabama Gulf Coast and Florida Panhandle within that corridor:
Dauphin Island · Fort Morgan · Gulf Shores · Orange Beach · Perdido Key · Pensacola Beach · Navarre Beach · Fort Walton Beach · Destin · Miramar Beach · 30A (Seaside, Rosemary Beach, WaterColor, etc.) · Panama City Beach

Do NOT write about: Texas beaches (Galveston, South Padre Island, Port Aransas), Mississippi Gulf Coast, or destinations east of PCB (Mexico Beach, St. George Island, Apalachicola, Cape San Blas).

## Content Production Notes

### Per-Post Requirements
Every new post MUST include:
- [ ] Post metadata added to `lib/posts.ts`
- [ ] Content component created in `app/blog/[slug]/content/[slug].tsx`
- [ ] Registered in CONTENT_MAP in `app/blog/[slug]/page.tsx`
- [ ] At least 2–3 `<AffiliateLink>` components (VRBO, Booking.com, TripShock/FishingBooker where relevant)
- [ ] At least 1–2 `<TipCallout>` components
- [ ] Internal links to related posts using `<Link href="/blog/...">`
- [ ] Internal links to destination pages using `<Link href="/destinations/...">`
- [ ] Proper H2/H3 heading structure (no skipping levels)
- [ ] Lead paragraph styled with `text-xl text-navy/80 leading-relaxed font-medium`
